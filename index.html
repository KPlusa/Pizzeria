About

A good pizzeria is not only about high-quality dishes. It is also a unique bond between service and customers. We have been putting our hearts into the dishes we prepare since 1996, thanks to which our clients keep coming back to us. We took our first steps in business in our own pizzeria with 200 seats. This pizzeria quickly gained popularity. It was not uncommon for customers to wait to be seated. Our restaurant attracted the attention of visitors who expressed their willingness to open a restaurant of a similar nature. We are Pizza Friends because our restaurants are made by friends for friends.
